## What is Maths Explore?

Maths Explore is a webapp for engineering calculations. You place blocks of maths, text, and diagrams anywhere on paginated sheets (A4 or US Letter). Maths is evaluated in reading order against one shared scope, so results update everywhere the moment you change an input — like a spreadsheet, but written as real mathematical notation.

New accounts start with a **14-day free trial** of the Individual plan — no card required. Head to [Getting started](guides/getting-started/) to create an account and build your first calcsheet, then explore the guides in the sidebar for blocks, maths syntax, units, templates, and more.
